Account & Data Deletion Request

In accordance with Google Play developer policies and the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) of the Philippines, we provide a straightforward way for users to delete their account and associated data.

1. How to Request Account Deletion

Option A: Request in the App (Recommended)

Open the FiliHomes app, go to My Page > Account Settings > Delete Account, and confirm your request. Your account will be queued for deletion instantly.

Option B: Request via Email (Web-based Request)

If you have already uninstalled the app or cannot log in, you can request deletion by emailing our support team.

Email Support: boyds79@gmail.com

2. Data Deletion and Retention Policy

Data Category Action Taken Retention Period
Profile (Name, Email, Phone) Permanently Erased Immediately upon deletion
Bazaar & Community Posts Permanently Erased Immediately upon deletion
Visitor QR Passes & Logs Archived (Anonymized) 5 Years (Building security audit compliance)
Billing & Payment Records Archived (Anonymized) 5 Years (Philippine BIR audit compliance)
