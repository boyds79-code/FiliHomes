Privacy Policy

1. Compliance with RA 10173 (DPA)

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (DPA) of the Philippines, we are committed to safeguarding your personal data.

2. Data Minimization & Security

To protect your physical security inside the condo and prevent unsolicited door-to-door visits, your exact house/lot number is masked in the public feed, and user aliases are used for Bazaar trades.

3. Shared Data

Your data is stored securely on Supabase cloud servers. We do not sell, distribute, or share personal data with external third parties unless ordered by court order or local security investigations.

4. Data Retention Policy

Upon account deletion, all personal identifiers are permanently erased or anonymized. Billing details and visitor records are archived for 5 years to comply with Philippine Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR) audits.
